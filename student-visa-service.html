Student Visa Requirements

General requirements for most student visa applications

Academic Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Strong academic records
  • Test scores (SAT, ACT, IB, A-levels)
  • University admission letter
  • Proof of English proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL)

Financial & Documentation

  • Proof of financial support
  • Bank statements (6 months)
  • Sponsor documents (if applicable)
  • Valid passport (6+ months validity)
  • Medical examination certificate

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does student visa processing take?

Typically 2-8 weeks depending on the destination country. We help expedite the process where possible.

Can I work while studying?

Yes! Most countries allow 15-20 hours per week during studies and full-time during breaks. Requirements vary by country.

What if my application is rejected?

We provide free re-application assistance. We identify issues and strengthen your second application for higher success rates.

Can I bring my family?

Dependents can typically accompany you. Eligibility depends on your financial capacity and visa type.
