Requirements & Eligibility

General guidelines for visa applications

Common Requirements

  • Valid passport with 6+ months validity
  • Completed visa application form
  • Passport photographs (as per specifications)
  • Proof of financial capacity
  • Medical examination certificate
  • Police clearance certificate
  • Travel insurance (when applicable)
  • Supporting documents specific to visa type

Documents Required

  • Educational credentials and certificates
  • Employment letters and experience documents
  • Bank statements and financial proof
  • Spouse/dependent documents (if applicable)
  • Property ownership documents
  • Previous visa stamps and travel history
  • Invitation letters from employers/institutions
  • Vaccination records
